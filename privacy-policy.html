Privacy Policy

Your information stays simple, limited, and used only to respond.

This Privacy Policy explains how LineCraft Tile & Floors handles information submitted through this website or by email for tile, flooring, caulking, trim, and estimate requests.

LineCraft Tile & Floors is operated by Shafaliuk Construction Incorporated.

Information collected How it is used Sharing Contact

Last updated: July 13, 2026

1. Overview

LineCraft Tile & Floors, also referred to as Line Craft Tile and Floors, is operated by Shafaliuk Construction Incorporated (“LineCraft,” “we,” “our,” or “us”).

This website is designed to provide information about tile and flooring services and to let homeowners contact us for estimate requests. We do not operate customer accounts, online checkout, payment processing, advertising profiles, or a customer marketing database through this website.

2. Information we collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide when you contact us by email, phone, text, or the estimate form. This may include:

  • Your name;
  • Phone number;
  • Email address;
  • City or township;
  • Project type and preferred contact method;
  • Project details you choose to include;
  • Human verification response used to reduce spam form submissions.

Please do not send sensitive personal information, financial information, payment card numbers, Social Security numbers, or other information that is not needed for a tile or flooring inquiry.

3. How we use information

We use the information you provide only for practical business communication related to your request, including to:

  • Reply to your inquiry;
  • Discuss your tile, flooring, backsplash, caulking, trim, or repair project;
  • Schedule a call, visit, or estimate conversation;
  • Prepare or follow up on requested project information.

LineCraft does not use inquiry information for advertising databases, data brokerage, or unrelated marketing lists.

4. Limited form handling and inquiry storage

Estimate form submissions are handled through Netlify Forms, the website hosting and form-handling service used for this site. Submissions may be stored in the Netlify form submissions dashboard and/or delivered by email so LineCraft can review and respond to the request.

LineCraft does not use a separate customer portal, CRM database, advertising database, or data brokerage system for website inquiries. Email communications and form submissions may remain available long enough to respond to your request and manage normal project communication. We aim to keep only what is reasonably needed for communication, scheduling, estimates, or legal/business record purposes.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal inquiry information with advertising networks, data brokers, marketing lists, or unrelated companies.

Limited inquiry information may be processed by service providers that help operate the website, form submission system, hosting, or email delivery, including Netlify. We may also disclose information if required by law, to respond to lawful legal process, to protect our rights or safety, or with your direct permission.

6. Cookies, analytics, and third-party services

This website is intended to be a simple informational website. It does not intentionally use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, user accounts, or online payment tools.

The website may load basic assets such as fonts or images and may be hosted by a website hosting provider. Netlify, browsers, email providers, or similar technical services may process limited technical information as part of delivering the website, receiving form submissions, or sending email notifications. LineCraft does not use that information to create customer profiles.

7. Security

We use reasonable care to limit the information we request and to use it only for responding to inquiries. No email or internet transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure, so please avoid sending sensitive information that is not needed for your project request.

8. Your choices

You may contact us to ask about your inquiry information, request that we correct contact details, or ask us to delete email communication that is no longer needed, subject to any legal or business record requirements.

9. Children’s privacy

This website is intended for homeowners and adults requesting home improvement services. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the website, contact process, or privacy practices change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
