Helpful answers

Before the first tile is set.

Every home and material is different, but these answers help homeowners understand the usual next steps.

What types of tile and flooring projects do you handle?

LineCraft focuses on bathroom and shower tile, kitchen backsplashes, tile floors, wood and resilient flooring, basements, halls, caulking, trim, and detail finishing. Share the project type, approximate size, and current surface condition to confirm the best fit.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes. Start with the estimate form and include your town, project type, approximate size, and main concerns. Some projects can begin with a phone or email review; others require an in-person visit before final pricing.

Can you help me understand how much material to buy?

After the layout and site conditions are reviewed, LineCraft can help confirm practical quantities and overage. Final material purchasing responsibilities should be listed clearly in your estimate.

How long does a bathroom or flooring project take?

Timing depends on demolition, surface condition, waterproofing, tile size, pattern, cure times, room access, and finish work. You will receive a realistic project window after the scope is reviewed.

Which areas do you serve?

Service includes Philadelphia and nearby communities across Bucks and Montgomery Counties, including Southampton, Newtown, Doylestown, Yardley, Bensalem, Warminster, Horsham, and surrounding towns.

What details help before scheduling a visit?

The most helpful details are the room type, approximate size, current surface condition, preferred material, timeline, and any issues you already know about.
